## Problem

Campus communication is fragmented across email, flyers, social apps, and disconnected event tools.

## Approach

I worked on a unified platform architecture combining iOS client features, web/admin experiences, and a Python backend with asynchronous job processing and managed data services.

## Outcome

Spora integrates discovery, events, messaging, and organization tools in one product surface, with a clear path for multi-service deployment and scale-oriented operations.

## What this demonstrates

- End-to-end product engineering
- Cross-platform system design
- Delivery across mobile, backend, and web layers
